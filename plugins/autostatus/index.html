This Plugin Requires Autostatus.

Autostatus does not require any patches to work with XRMS.
Simple make these settings to your autostatus/config file:
statusfile /PATH/TO/autostatus/local.status
hostfile /PATH/TO/autostatus/hosts
baseurl https://SERVER.DOMAIN.COM/xrms/plugins/autostatus
iconurl https://SERVER.DOMAIN.COM/xrms/plugins/autostatus/icons
outpage /PATH/TO/xrms/plugins/autostatus/index.html
email YOUR@EMAIL.COM
emerg YOUR@EMAIL.COM
content_subject on
  • You may need to change "https" to "http"
  • Be sure to copy the icons directory to xrms/plugins/autostatus/icons
